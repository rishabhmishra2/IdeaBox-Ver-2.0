About IdeaBox
“We have approximately 60,000 thoughts in a day. Unfortunately, 95% of them are thoughts we had the day before.”
There's a saying, “Nothing changes if nothing changes.” Well if nothing changes, we stay the same. We don’t grow. We don’t evolve.

Innovation is what got us here and Innovation is what will spur our growth and take us far. IdeaBox is a platform to facilitate and accelerate this Innovation within the company. IdeaBox is the one stop place for all the new ideas across product features, platform, tools, strategic acquisitions and process improvements. So, go ahead and don't let that idea just stay within you!
